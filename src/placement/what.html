what we do

OBJECTIVE:

To Make our Engineers future ready.

What we do:

The Department interacts with reputed organizations all over the country and arranges campus placement for final year students.
It makes effort to send students for in-plant training, Internship and Summer Project.
We arrange for Guest lectures and Industrial visits to develop their technical ability and gain practical knowledge.
We conduct Soft Skills training to build the students personality and make them a successful professional
Technical symposiums and club activities are held regularly to make students industry ready.

Why Velammal Institute of Technology:

Our Strengths

Velammal I tech offers the highest number of students meeting the eligibility criteria for recruitment, as we have the highest pass percentage in University examinations
Best Placement ratio (. No. of students attended vs. No. of students recruited) in most of the recruiting companies
We have a common in house soft skills training team for velammal group of engineering colleges to train the students on spoken English and personality development. Our team of soft skill trainers
Provides comprehensive training in the areas of placement preparedness, communication skills and personality development, thus empowering our students with knowledge skill and attitude required for a secure future.
Post placement training to students to make them ‘Plug & Play’ in industry in the areas of Technical knowledge and Personality traits
